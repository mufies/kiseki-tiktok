Test your RTMP to HLS transcoding with Adaptive Bitrate Streaming

Quick Test Guide

  1. Make sure stream service is running on port 8083
  2. Start streaming from OBS to: rtmp://localhost:1935/live/{stream_key}
  3. Enter your stream ID below (from database)
  4. Click "Load Stream"
  5. Wait 6-12 seconds for first HLS segments to be generated
